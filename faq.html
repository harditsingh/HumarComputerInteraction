Q01. How do I make a purchase of an item?

You only have to go the required product and press on the cart icon beneath it so it will be added to the shopping cart. Once the item is added, you can either continue shopping or go to the shopping cart to finish the payment and the product(s) will be shipped for you as soon as possible.

Q02. Do I need to set up an account to place an order?

To place an order, it is better to make an account. That will make it easier to track the order in an advance step. Through your account you can request a “Return an item” in case you were not satisfied with what you recieved.

Q03. What should I do if I forgot my password?

Beneath the “type your password” field there is an icon says: “Forgot my password”. You can click on it and follow the instructions and a link to reset your password will be sent to your registered email. The link will guide you to reset your password.

Q04. Which size should I choose for my child?

Our products were made depending on the european measurements sizes. An addition index for the sizes and measurements is provided in every product description.

Q05. What kind of payments could I use on the webshop, and are they safe?

To keep our customers secured and satisfied, we are using the latest secure payment methods. Mastercards/ Visa Cards/ Maestro and ideal all are accepted. (((For further details, please see the Privacy Policy section.))))

Q06. When I expect my products to be delivered to my address?

We are proud to say that time of delivery within the European countries is no more than 5 working days.

Q07. Is it possible to change the shipping address after completing my order?

Unfortunately it is not possible to change the shipping address once the payment is completed. In that case you should contact us via our live chat team to cancel the order and make a new order with the new address. You should wait 24 hours so the old order will be canceled and the money will be returned to the same account within 7 working days depending on the international money transactions.

Q08. Can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email of your shipping details and a tracking number. If you have registered, you will be able to view and track the status of your shipment by signing in and selecting 'My Account' followed by 'Orders'.

Q09. Am I going to need an app - as well as the T-shirt - to be warned on time?

No. You will not need to look at any device to be warned on time that you are about to get sunburnt. The T-shirt sufficies completely because it changes its color when the situation is not yet too critical, so that you can be safe.
