Who are we?

A technology company dedicated to developing next generation smart clothing. We go beyond being a brand. We’re a movement revolutionising the interactions between people and everyday wear. Our innovative clothing range warns users that they are in risk of getting sunburnt. Using specially developed smart fabric, our products detect dangerous UV rays and warn you via a clear colour change. The warning system also takes into account both your skin type and the time it takes you to burn, and doesn’t require phones, apps or electronics.
